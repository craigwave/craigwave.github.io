Craig Stubbs-Race

video editor | filmmaker | media artist

craig_phua@hotmail.com | @craigwave

Currently in my final year at UNSW Art and Design studying a Bachelor of Media Arts (Honours). I plan on working with video production mediums and computer generated effects and animations. I want to create experimental narratives so there is some possibility of learning new technologies to achieve what I am after.

My exploration into media arts began with simple Adobe Flash animations in high school. From there I completed a Certificate IV in Graphic Design at TAFE and then entered UNSW Art and Design to do a Bachelor of Media Arts (Honours). During my time at university I have completed numerous digital media internships and worked on several side projects.

Documentaries and arthouse films are the foundations of my passionate for filmmaking. They are the rawest and most authentic stories to tell. They can either change the way you think about your own physical world or challenge your own sense of creativity and storytelling. The only way to grow as artists is to constantly challenge.
