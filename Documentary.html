Painting With Pixels

2016

A short film to learn about the art and process behind VJing. I only learnt about the practice through a friend and decided to follow him around documenting the work he did. Through the project I was able to reach out and contact other VJer's and learnt a lot about the other people involved in a live gig. The project was a great opportunity to refine my filmmaking and editing skills.

This was the first time I was able to incorporate an actual sense of story into one of my documentaries. I learnt the importance of needing a strong narrative that is compelling to watch. For future projects I plan to focus on my storytelling abilities.

Girls

2016

We were tasked with creating a short documentary for a university assignment. We chose to research a Girls Hip Hop night at Hudson Ballroom (formally Plan B Club) in Sydney and asked one of the founders, Olivia Suleimon (@yemisul) for an interview. Building from the knowledge I had from the Martina documentary our group had a focus in achieving good cinematography and a strong interview, both of which we achieved.

Scribbles 'n' Sportswear

2014

First short documentary/interview that Sabrina Calero and I created in an attempt to learn more about filmmaking. Martina Calvi (@martinamartian) was more than generous enough to let us document her creative process and follow her around.

Through this project I learnt the fundamentals of shooting an interview and the importance of B roll. Upon reflection I wish there was a consistent colour palette and more interesting backgrounds.
