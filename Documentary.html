Painting With Pixels

2016

A short film to learn about the art and process behind VJing. I only learnt about the practice through a friend and decided to follow him around documenting the work he did. Through the project I was able to reach out and contact other VJer's and learnt a lot about the other people involved in a live gig. This project was a great opportunity to refine my filmmaking and interview skills.

Girls

2016

University assignment for Video Document class. We chose to research a Girls Hip Hop night at a club in Sydney and asked one of the founders Olivia Suleimon (@yemisul) for an interview.
